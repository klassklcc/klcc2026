Rules & Guidelines

The prefects behind the KL Coding Cup 2026 will attempt their best at maintaining integrity and fair and clean competition, to ensure this the competitors must follow the rules and guideliens:

  • AI chatbots such as DeepSeek, GitHub Copilot or ChatGPT are not allowed, as all teams are required to make the small intuitive leaps to answer difficult questions on their own.

  • Misconduct with other teams is prohibited. This includes answer sharing between same school teams, and general bad manners towards any team.

  • Searching the internet for issues is allowed. Stack Overflow usage will be allowed during the competition, but do not copy and paste code to get your answers. We will know.
