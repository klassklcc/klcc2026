Final Round

You have finally bypassed the second vault. However, you discover that the entire system is split into N terminals. To reboot the system and revert the changes you must link terminals together using Data Cables
You have managed to recover a set of M timestamped logs. These logs show that two terminals were linked at a specific timestamp.
The system will only reboot if three specific "Core Terminals" are part of the same connected network. You must find the earliest timestamp at which these three terminals became interconnected.


Example Question

Input style:
a b c (terminals to sync)
N M (total terminals and the length of the logs)
T1 A1 B1 (these follow the structure (time, Terminal A, Terminal B)
T2 A2 B2
...
TM AM BM

Example input:
1 4 6
7 6
100 1 2
150 5 6
200 4 5
250 2 7
300 1 3
350 3 4
Output: 350
Explanation: At time 350, it connects {1, 2, 3, 7} and {4, 5, 6} which results in {1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7}, and is the only one where 1, 4, and 6 are connected.
